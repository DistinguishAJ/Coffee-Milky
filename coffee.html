About Us Our Story

Coffee Milky began with a simple craving: to make barista-quality, creamy coffee as easy to get as your morning paper.
What started as a weekend pop-up has grown into a team of passionate coffee lovers who care about every step of the cup — from selecting responsibly roasted beans to perfecting the milk texture that gives our drinks their signature velvet mouthfeel.
We believe great coffee should be both approachable and exceptional, so we focus on balanced recipes that highlight origin flavors while keeping that comforting, milky finish customers love.
We work closely with small roasters and farmers to source beans that are flavorful and ethically produced, and our baristas train to bring consistency and care to every order.
Whether it’s a classic latte, a creamy cold brew, or one of our seasonal specialties, each drink is crafted to deliver a smooth, satisfying experience.
Fast, reliable delivery is part of our promise your favorite cup arrives fresh and ready to enjoy, with minimal fuss.
More than coffee, Coffee Milky is about making mornings cozier and routines a little kinder.
We’re committed to sustainable practices where we can, friendly service every day, and building a community of coffee lovers who appreciate warmth in every sip.

Why Choose Us?

We take pride in producing our coffee using ethically sourced beans from trusted farmers, combined with advanced roasting techniques that lock in freshness and flavor. Every step of our production process is guided by strict quality control and a strong commitment to sustainability, ensuring each cup delivers excellence.

Distribution

Our wide-reaching distribution network is designed to serve both small businesses and large partners with ease. By maintaining consistent stock levels and building strong relationships across the market, we ensure our coffee is always available without interruptions, no matter the demand.

Delivery

We make freshness a priority by offering fast, efficient, and reliable delivery options. With careful packaging, real-time tracking, and flexible scheduling, our delivery service guarantees that every order arrives in perfect condition, ready to be enjoyed.

Products

Our product range is crafted with variety and innovation at its core. From classic blends to smooth milky options and seasonal specialties, we offer something for every coffee lover.
